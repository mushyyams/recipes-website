This started as a salad topper and became a standalone obsession. The secret isn't the spices — it's **drying the chickpeas like your life depends on it**.

## The dryness test

After patting dry, let them sit on the counter for ten minutes while the oven preheats. Any residual moisture steams instead of crisps.

## Make them your own

Swap cayenne for gochugaru for a fruitier heat. Add grated parmesan in the last five minutes for something saltier and more bar-snack coded.
