There are two special places in the world that have a hypnotic grip on my subconscious- Chinese Bakeries and Auntie Anne's. Both establishments have the unique quality of saturating the air around them with the hypnotic smell of butter, yeast, and sugar that can lull any passerby into their arms. They share the same fable-likeness as the sirens, luring sailors in with their songs, but rather than being dragged into the ocean to meet my demise, I come out on top, enjoying a fluffy, buttery treat. 

Though they can usually be found in places of congregation, I've found both places hold a feeling of isolation that I can only describe as liminal.
